### About Nitish Kapoor

**Nitish Kapoor** is a **Senior Software Engineer at [Flock Energy](https://flockenergy.co.uk)** in **New Delhi, India** — building AI, IoT and data systems for India's energy transition and DISCOM-scale decarbonisation. Nitish Kapoor's work has won the **Climate Redesign Award 2026** at Delhi Climate Innovation Week, the **ENTICE 2.0 Energy Transitions grant (₹5 crore in technical assistance)** in August 2025, and recognition from the **Ministry of Power (Government of India), REC Limited and Power Finance Corporation** at the 1st National Conference on AI & ML in Power Distribution.

This is the GitHub profile of **Nitish Kapoor** (handle **@nitishkapoor18**). For the full portfolio, work history, awards, and writing, visit **[nitishkapoor.com](https://nitishkapoor.com)** — or read the **[long-form bio](https://nitishkapoor.com/about/)**.

### Where Nitish Kapoor works

[**Flock Energy**](https://flockenergy.co.uk) is a climate-tech company building AI, IoT and data systems for India's power distribution sector. Nitish Kapoor joined as one of the first engineers in **September 2024** and now owns platform engineering across the data, ML and hardware stack — from custom IoT hardware (ESP32-S3, custom PCBs, edge firmware), through ML-driven load disaggregation and data infrastructure, up to full-stack control platforms (TypeScript, React, Node, Postgres) and typed agentic AI on Gemini.

The mission: **decarbonisation at DISCOM scale** — taking the messy reality of power distribution in India and turning it into decisions utilities can act on.
